Our Institute

Future Institute is Uzbekistan awarding body, located in Tashkent. It was established in 2021 to spread the light of the knowledge. It implements the best practices of higher education all over the world, especially from developed countries. Future Institute cooperates with prestigious universities which are at the TOP rankings.

The objectives of the LEVEL 3 International Foundation Diploma for Higher Education Studies are to:

  • provide career path support to learners who wish to develop their management skills, enterprise capabilities and opportunities in their chosen sector
  • improve learner understanding of any given business environments and organizations and how they are managed and developed
  • develop skills and abilities in learners to support their professional development.
  • provide an entry route to international university courses.
Learn More

Why Future Insitute

Our qualifications provide a rich mix of disciplines and skills development opportunities. Learners will gain insight into the functioning, objectives and processes of organizations, appreciating their diversity and the influences and impact of external forces on them. The fast-changing and complex business environment and different organizational ability to stay resilient and respond positively to change and opportunities will be explored.
